Our Founder

Người sáng lập của AAS

Meet our founder, Francis Couture, fondly dubbed ‘Big Daddy’. With over 25 years in microbiology and biotechnology, he specializes in fermentation and microorganism characterization. His contributions span 10 countries and encompass agriculture, human and animal health, bio-pesticides, and lab services. Since 2004, Francis has been an entrepreneur in Vietnam, designing a 2,000-liter fermentation process in Phu My and launching a dedicated R&D biotech center in 2016 exclusively exploiting the power of active microorganisms. His vision and expertise are the driving forces behind our company’s success.

What Mr. Francis have to say!

Microbiology and biotechnology are set to play a transformative role in Vietnam, catalyzing advancements in agriculture, human and animal health, and environmental sustainability. As we explore the rich potential of the microbial world, it’s crucial for both individuals and businesses to discern genuine biotech R&D firms from simple bacteria producers. This differentiation is vital to harness the full power of microorganisms and make informed decisions, to benefit fully from their potential, whether personally or commercially. At AAS, we stand by our commitment to deliver results, backed by rigorous research, validation and a dedication to making a positive impact.
